How Synk Works

Say the plan. Synk understands intent, synchronizes all apps, and manages the journey end-to-end.

1. Understand

You speak naturally. Synk processes intent, locations, times, preferences, and calendar context.

2. Plan

Synk finds the best options across flights, cabs, hotels & stays — then builds one synchronized itinerary.

3. Execute

Bookings, reschedules, re-timing for delays, and all transports are handled automatically.

4. Pay

Synk’s smart wallet picks the right card, applies offers, and stores receipts without you doing anything.
